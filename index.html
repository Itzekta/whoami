Pineapple Chicken Curry

This is a Thai dish that is full of delicious flavors and nutrients. Itâ€™s seriously divine!

Ingredients for this recipe:

  • 1 quart water
  • 2 cups raw jasmine rice
  • 1/4 cup red curry paste
  • 2 cans of coconut milk
  • Fresh pineapples chunks
  • 1 cup of sliced bamboo shoots
  • 2 boneless chopped chicken breast halves
  • Vegetables such as Tomatoes and bell peppers
  • 3 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Thai Basil

How to make this dish?

  1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a pot. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes.
  2. Add curry paste and 3/4 can of coconut milk to a medium pot over high heat and stir together until a creamy sauce forms for about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a wok and mix in remaining chicken, bamboo shoots, sugar, and fish sauce. Bring to a boil and cook until chicken juices run clear, 20 minutes.
  4. Mix bell peppers and onion into the wok. Continue cooking until peppers are tender, about 10 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat, add the Thai basil and pineapple, and stir in one last time.
  6. Serve over cooked jasmine rice.
